Take me home Tour 2013

At the 23 February 2013 Josh Devine started as the Drummer from One Direction the Take me home Tour 2013. While the Tour, they have Concerts in European, America, Asia, and Australia. While the Tour, they also have they’re first concert in Switzerland, at the 16 may 2013 in Zurich.

Madison Square Garden

At December 5. 2012 Josh Devine occur as drummer from the Band One Direction at the Madison Square Garden in New Yok. In a record time from less than one minute the 20‘000 Tickets was solded. But, if you can't take a Ticket you had one more chance: in 40 diffrent Country (also in Switzerland) were give away 2 Go1Den Tickets per country.

Better with you tour

From the 17. February 2012 to the 10. February 2010, Josh Devine was as the drummer from One Direction, the opening act for the Better with U tour from the American Boygroup Big Time Rush. The Concert centred only on North America
