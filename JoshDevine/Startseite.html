Josh Devine is a british Session Drummer.
Currently he's working for the british boyband One Direction.
Josh discover Aged 3 years his passion for the drumset
2007 he won 2 Nationale Drummer competition (at Beat 2007 & Mapex Drummer of Tomorrow)
Mapex let Josh in the Category "18-25 of the European Drummer" from Europe Drummer of Tomorrow enter, even though he was only 16 years old!
